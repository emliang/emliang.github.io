Enming Liang

This is Enming Liang (梁恩明). Currently, I am a third-year Ph.D. student (2021-) in school of data science, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Minghua Chen. I received my bachelor degree in School of Intelligent Systems Engineering from Sun Yat-sen University in 2020, supervised by Prof. Renxin Zhong. My research interests include machine learning for optimization, and its application for transportation and power systems.
