Enming Liang



This is Enming Liang (梁恩明). I received Ph.D. (2021-2024) in Department of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Minghua Chen. I received my bachelor degree (2016-2020) in School of Intelligent Systems Engineering from Sun Yat-sen University. supervised by Prof. Renxin Zhong.
I have been a member of the DeepOPF team, working with Prof. Steven Low, focusing on applying machine learning approach for power gird operation. I had been a visiting student to department of CST, University of Cambridge, supervised by Prof. Srinivasan Keshav.
I had worked as a research intern in MSRA (Beijing), Huawei Noah's Ark Lab (Shenzhen), and DiDi-SYSU Research Project (Guangzhou). I had contributed to tutorials in the Climate Change AI summer school, working with Prof. Priya L. Donti.
My research interests include Machine Learning for Constrained Optimization, and its application for Mobility, Energy, and Climate.
