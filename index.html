Enming Liang

This is Enming Liang (梁恩明). I received Ph.D. (2021-2024) in Department of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Minghua Chen. I received my bachelor degree (2016-2020) in School of Intelligent Systems Engineering from Sun Yat-sen University, supervised by Prof. Renxin Zhong. My research interests include Machine Learning for Optimization, and its application for Mobility, Energy, and Climate.
